A simple CDONTS Example

 <%
  Dim objMail
  Set objMail = Server.CreateObject("CDONTS.NewMail")
  
  EM = request.form("email")
  
     objMail.From = "rtaylor@aspalliance.com"
     objMail.Subject = "How TO send email with CDONTS"
     objMail.To = EM
     objMail.Body = "This is an email message" & vbcrlf&_ 
     "with CDONTS." & vbcrlf&_
     "This article can be found here " & vbcrlf&_
     "http://www.aspadvice.com/cdonts/cdonts.asp" 
     objMail.Send
 
 'You must always do this with CDONTS.  You should 
 'do it anyway.
 'objMail.close
  set objMail = nothing
  
  
 Response.write("Mail was Sent")
 
 
  %>
